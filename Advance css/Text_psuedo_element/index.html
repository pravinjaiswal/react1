Pravin Jaiswal also known as Singer.New York Film Academy (NYFA), named by Variety as a Top Film School in 2020, is a premier visual and performing arts college offering accredited degrees and non-degree programs in numerous areas of study, including Acting for Film. Programs are offered Online and On-Campus in New York (NYC), Miami, Los Angeles (LA), and locations around the world.


Our innovative acting for film programs are designed for a new generation of storytellers. With an unrivaled approach to hands-on, interactive learning, students find themselves completely immersed in their course of study from day one. Classes are taught by award-winning industry professionals, in a real-world environment with state-of-the-art facilities and equipment.
